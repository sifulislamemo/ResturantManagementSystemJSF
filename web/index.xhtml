about

The original S I Restaurant, celebrating 05 years of operation, features several private party room that can accommodate groups up to 40 guests in each room, in addition to its cozy living room with its wood burning fireplace. The sidewalk café is perfect for Chelsea people watching.

S I Restaurant offers a seasonally updated menu with a large selection of starters, soups, salads and entrees from its Contemporary Bangladeshi menu that will please the most discriminating palate. Special dietary requests are welcome and accommodated, including low-calorie and vegetarian dishes.

Open until 12:00 pm daily, serving food until 10:00 pm.
